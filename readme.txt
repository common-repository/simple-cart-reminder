== Description ==
Remind customers to complete their shopping cart.
Statistics have shown that mostly customers are disturbed while they shopping and they forget to complete their shopping cart.
As a result, your shop actually lose 20% of sales.
Simple cart reminder solves the problem and bring your customer back with a reminding email.


### Important Notice:
- Plugin works based on WooCommerce 3.x plugin.

== Installation ==

1. Unzip the download package
1. Upload `simple-cart-reminder` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
